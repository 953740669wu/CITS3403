    Medical care and contraception for pets

    Both cats and dogs need regular medical care, including vaccinations, deworming, and bathing. Prompt visits and following the vet's recommendations are essential to their health. In order to avoid excessive stray animals and health problems, spaying and neutering cats and dogs is necessary. This not only helps control animal populations, but also helps improve their behavior and health.

    →

    Dietary Restrictions

    Cats and dogs have their own food taboos. For example, dogs can't eat foods such as chocolate, onions, garlic, and cats are allergic to dairy products and can't eat foods such as onions and chocolate. In additon, Citrus (oranges, lemons, tangerines) are acidic fruits, although rich in vitamin C, but also stimulate the cat's stomach, if used in excess, it is easy to cause vomiting or diarrhea.

    Dog species

    Learn more about our lovely dogs. Here are some dog types and descriptions.

    Siberian Husky

    The Siberian Husky is a medium-sized breed that resembles a wolf in appearance, with a thick double coat. They often have blue or brown eyes, and sometimes one of each. Huskies are friendly, energetic, and love to exercise. They are well-suited to cold climates but do not do well when left alone for long periods.

    Border Collie

    The Border Collie is a medium-sized breed known for its intelligence and working ability. They are lively, energetic dogs that require plenty of exercise and mental stimulation. Friendly and adaptable, Border Collies make excellent working dogs as well as family pets. They excel in herding and various canine sports such as frisbee and agility competitions.

    Golden Retriever

    The Golden Retriever is a large breed with a golden coat, known for its gentle and friendly nature. They are loyal, intelligent, and easily trainable, making them suitable for roles such as guide dogs and search and rescue dogs. They are affectionate companions and are great with families.

    Pomeranian

    The Pomeranian is a small breed with a cute appearance and dense fur. They are lively, confident, and sometimes a bit stubborn. Adapted to city life, they enjoy being close to their owners. Regular grooming is necessary to keep their coat tidy.

    English Foxhound

    The English Foxhound is a large breed with well-developed muscles, suitable for long outdoor activities. They have a friendly personality, strong social skills, and get along well with other dogs. Bred for hunting, especially tracking and fox hunting.

    Shiba Inu

    The Shiba Inu is a medium-sized breed originating from Japan, known for its compact appearance and upright ears. They have an independent and confident personality, with a high level of alertness. Requiring moderate exercise, they enjoy exploration and adventure. They are loyal to their owners but may be reserved with strangers.

    Cat breeds

    Explore our adorable cats. Here are some cat breeds and descriptions.

    Siamese Cat

    The Siamese cat is a medium-sized breed known for its striking appearance and vocal nature. They have a slender body, blue almond-shaped eyes, and short coat with color points. Siamese cats are social, intelligent, and enjoy interacting with their owners.

    Maine Coon

    The Maine Coon is a large and majestic breed with a long, bushy tail and tufted ears. They are affectionate, friendly, and often described as "gentle giants." Maine Coons are good with children and other pets, making them great family companions.

    Persian Cat

    The Persian cat is known for its long, luxurious coat and distinctive flat face. They have a calm and gentle demeanor, making them ideal indoor companions. Persian cats require regular grooming to maintain their coat's health and appearance.

    Bengal Cat

    The Bengal cat is a medium to large-sized breed known for its distinctive leopard-like spots and athletic build. They are energetic, playful, and highly intelligent. Bengal cats enjoy interactive play and mental stimulation.

    Ragdoll Cat

    The Ragdoll cat is a large and affectionate breed with striking blue eyes and a semi-long coat. They are known for their relaxed and gentle nature, often going limp when picked up. Ragdolls are good with children and other pets.

    Scottish Fold

    The Scottish Fold cat is characterized by its unique folded ears, giving it an adorable and distinctive appearance. They are known for their sweet and affectionate nature, often forming strong bonds with their owners. Scottish Folds are adaptable to various living environments.
